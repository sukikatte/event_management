title Sequence Diagram: 03 View Account (Administrator, Organiser or User Viewing Their Account)
actor "Administrator/ Organizer/ User" as a
participantspacing 4
control "<link:_>: AccountController" as s
participant "<link:_>: AccountService" as r
entity "<link:_>: Account" as ac


==1. The administrator, organiser or user selects the own account functionality on the main page for their account type **(01-main-admin, 02-main-organiser, 03-main-user)**==

a->s:viewAccountDetail()
activate s

s->r:getAccount(type, name)
activate r


r->r:findAccount(type, name)

r->ac:v:=getAccount()
r-->s:return account
deactivate r

s->s:displayAccountPage()

s-->a:use case finished!
deactivate s

==2. The system displays the view account page **(16-profile-admin, 21-profile-organiser, 08-profile-user)** which shows the details of the selected account==
